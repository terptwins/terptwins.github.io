Lazy Jane

"Take Lazy Jane for a spin and see how easy watering and training with a trellis can be!"

Low Profile

When controlling the height of your canopy is everything, Lazy Jane's 3 inch profile allows for incredible convenience without stealing heavily from your vertical allowance.

More Info

  • Lazy Jane's all wood top and base are both 1 inch thick to ensure durability while staying very low to the ground.

  • The Lazy Jane 12 inch ball bearing turntable adds an additional 3/8 of an inch bring Lazy Janes finaly height to 2 and 3/8 inches.

  • Lazy Jane gives you the convenience of easily feeding and rotating your plants without your canopy space suffering.
Built to last!

Lazy Janes rotating wooden base is organically sealed and wrapped in Mylar to ensure UV and moisture protection. Lazy Jane's plastic trellis frame is designed to be used again and again for supporting and training your canopy.

More Info

  • Lazy Jane's rotating base is made of kiln dried pine and organically sealed with boiled linseed oil to ensure the longevity of the build.

  • Lazy Jane's 12 inch ball bearing turntable is zinc plated to help resist corrosion and has a maximum rating of 1000lbs.

  • Lazy Jane's trellis is made of a hard plastic polymer and wrapped in mylar tape to protect it from the environment.
Convenience Meets Efficiency

Lazy Jane's innovative circular trellis allows your canopy to be tied down while maintaining the mobility and convenience the turntables provide for feeding.

More Info

Plants can be difficult to maneuver and tend to when they are engulfed in a standard square trellis. Lazy Jane's trellis is anchored in the center and rotates with the turntable allowing you to freely rotate your plants and feed them with ease while still giving you the ability to tied down your canopy to fully utilize the space you are growing in.
